2019 HALO Luncheon

HomeAid Houston's annual HALO luncheon provides a unique and intimate look at homelessness in our community and shares, through first-hand accounts, its impact on people of all ages. The luncheon brings together successful women who are passionate about our community and our cause, including executive directors, clients of agencies we serve, and speakers who have experienced the difficulties and challenges of homelessness.

     

Our theme for 2019 is New Beginnings. The luncheon will highlight HomeAid Houston's projects and initiatives that are building new lives for Houston's homeless families and individuals. We will also hear from several speakers who will share their inspiring stories as they transitioned out of crisis situations and are realizing New Beginnings. 

 

The Westin - Memorial City

945 Gessner Road

Friday, November 1, 2019

11:00: Networking & Registration

11:30 - 1:00: Luncheon & Program
