2019 Project Playhouse

  We are pleased to announce our collaboration with

Satterfield & Pontikes Construction and Newberry Architecture  

 on this year's Project Playhouse, playfully named "The Toy Box".

The playhouse will be on display during Astros home games

in Section 150 from June 25th - July 7th at Minute Maid Park.

Raffle tickets will be sold at all home games listed and are 

also available in the link above. Drawing on Sunday, July 7th.

 

For more information or sponsorship opportunities,

contact Kirsten Robbins at krobbins@homeaidhouston.org
