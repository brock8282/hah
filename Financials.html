HomeAid Houston is a 501(c)(3) organization that offers a tax deduction for gifts received. That means your contributions are fully tax-deductible to the allowed amount, allowing you to make the most of your contributions. HomeAid Houston relies on generous donors like you and graciously asks for your continued support.
